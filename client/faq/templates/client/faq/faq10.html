For the Basic RiderCourse and Kickstart course, students will use our training motorcycles. For the 3-Wheeled Basic RiderCourse and the Skilled RiderCourse students can use their own motorcycles.
Students who plan on only riding a scooter (not a moped), can bring their own bike to use during the Basic RiderCourse. However, this must be pre-approved and will need to be in good working order and comply with all state laws for safety requirements to be safely operated on a public roadway. You must also provide proof of ownership, insurance, and registration. You will be held personally and financially liable for any damages done to or by your machine.
