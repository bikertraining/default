ePackage-1 eCourse Registration Instructions

The MSF ePackage-1 eCourse must be completed before attending your scheduled class. Clicking on the button at the bottom of this page will redirect you to the Motorcycle Safety Foundation website to register and begin the eCourse.
Please follow the instructions below to register and begin the ePackage-1 eCourse.
To waive the $39.99 registration fee, you MUST enroll in the online eCourse using the button below. The course fee will be $0.00 if you use the unique link.
  • Enter your Student Information in the required fields.
  • Click Enroll Student at the bottom of the page.
  • If you have entered all required enrollment information, near the bottom of the page you will see a message in light green that says, "Temporary enrollee successfully added…."
  • Scroll down to the Waiver(s) and Indemnification(s).
  • IF YOU ARE ASKED TO MAKE A CREDIT CARD PAYMENT, STOP!
    The fee is $0.00 when you register using the link below. MSF does NOT provide refunds if you use your credit card instead of your enrollment link.
  • Read the Student Release(s) in full by scrolling to the bottom of the inset document, then check the I Accept box. The checkbox will not activate until you have scrolled to the end of the release.
  • Enter your email address in both required confirmation fields and click Complete Registration.
  • Read the information on the reservation confirmation screen and double-check the email address in the Student Fees section.
The course is designed to save your progress and allow you to complete it over multiple sessions.
Upon completion, you will be redirected to the course menu page (with the red “Click Here” button). Scroll down and click Completion Certificate. Email your completion certificate to ecourse@bikertraining.com
