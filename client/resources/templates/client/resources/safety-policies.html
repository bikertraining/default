Safety Policies

Required Gear

  • DOT‑approved helmet
  • Long sleeves & long pants
  • Full‑finger gloves
  • Sturdy over-the-ankle footwear

Health & Fitness

Students must be physically capable of safely operating a motorcycle. Please notify instructors of any medical conditions.
Must be able to balance a 2-wheel bicycle if taking the Basic RiderCourse or Skilled RiderCourse.

Substance Policy

Students may not participate under the influence of alcohol, drugs, or impairing medications. Violations result in immediate dismissal.

Equipment Use

School motorcycles must be operated responsibly. Damage caused by reckless and willful behavior may result in repair fees.
