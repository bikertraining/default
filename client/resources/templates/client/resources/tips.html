Essential Riding Tips

1. Look Where You Want to Go

Your motorcycle follows your eyes — keep your vision up and ahead.

2. Smooth Inputs = Smooth Riding

Throttle, clutch, and braking should be controlled and deliberate.

3. Master Slow‑Speed Control

Practice tight turns, balance, and clutch‑throttle coordination.

4. Always Ride Defensively

Assume other drivers may not see you — stay alert and visible.

5. Relax

It doesn't need to be perfect but you do need to maintain full control.

6. Have fun

Operating a motorcycle should be enjoyable.
