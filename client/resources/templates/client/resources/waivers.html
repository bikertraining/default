Student Waivers

All students must sign a liability waiver before participating in any training session. Motorcycle riding involves inherent risks, and while our instructors prioritize safety, students acknowledge responsibility for their own actions and decisions during training.
Parents or guardians must sign waivers for students under 18 years old.
