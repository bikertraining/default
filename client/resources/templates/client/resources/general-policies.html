General Policies

Attendance

Students must arrive at least 15 minutes before class begins. Late arrivals may not be allowed to participate for safety reasons.

Age Requirements

Riders must be at least 16 years old. Minors require a parent or guardian signature.

Identification

A valid state‑issued driver's license or learners permit is required for all training sessions and skill evaluations.

Weather Conditions

Training continues in the rain. Severe weather may require adjustments to be made for safety.
